what is the cosmere?

The Cosmere is a vast, interconnected fictional universe created by Brandon Sanderson, where many of his fantasy books take place. While each series or book can be read independently, they share a deeper underlying lore, with common themes, magic systems, and even recurring characters who travel between worlds.
Over time, connections between books become more apparent, rewarding readers who explore the entire universe.
